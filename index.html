Privacy Policy

This app ("CareU: Family Health Journal") helps users record and manage personal and family health data for personal reference only.

Information We Collect

All health data (such as body temperature, blood pressure, and personal notes) is entered by the user and stored solely on their own device. We, as the developer, do not collect, access, or process any health data you enter.

How We Use Information

The information is used solely by you, the user, for displaying and managing your personal health records within the app. The developer has no access to this data at any time.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you are signed into iCloud, your data is synced privately through your own Apple iCloud account via CloudKit. The developer cannot access, view, or retrieve your data at any time.

Optional Account Login

Sign in with Apple is available solely for managing an in-app subscription. It is not required to use the app's health features. No health data is associated with your account.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties.

Permissions

Camera: Used to capture images related to health notes.

Photo Library: Used to upload images related to health notes.

Disclaimer

This app is for personal record-keeping only and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.
